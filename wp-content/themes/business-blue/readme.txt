Business Blue WordPress theme.

== Description ==

Business Blue is Multipurpose, Responsive, SEO Friendly and child theme of Di Business theme. it comes with default blue color.

== Installation == 

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Type in 'Business Blue' in the search form and press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.
3. Click on 'Install' and then 'Activate' button to use your new theme right away.
4. Navigate to Appearance > Customize in your admin panel and customize the taste.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where can i locate theme options? =
Navigate to Appearance > Customize in your admin panel.
